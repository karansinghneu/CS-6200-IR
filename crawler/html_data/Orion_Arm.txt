It is also referred to by its full name, the <b>Orion&#8211;Cygnus Arm</b>, as well as <b>Local Arm</b>, <b>Orion Bridge</b>, and formerly, the <b>Local Spur</b> and <b>Orion Spur</b>.\n</p>\n<div Egypt</div></div></div>\n<p>The arm is named for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Orion_constellation" title="Orion constellation">Orion constellation</a>, which is one of the most prominent constellations of Northern Hemisphere winter (Southern Hemisphere summer).  Some of the brightest stars and most famous celestial objects of the constellation (e.g. <a href="/wiki/Betelgeuse" title="Betelgeuse">Betelgeuse</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rigel" title="Rigel">Rigel</a>, the three stars of <a href="/wiki/Orion%27s_Belt" title="Orion\'s Belt">Orion\'s Belt</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Orion_Nebula" title="Orion Nebula">Orion Nebula</a>) are within it as shown on the <a href="#Interactive_maps">interactive map below</a>.\n</p><p>The arm is between the <a href="/wiki/Carina%E2%80%93Sagittarius_Arm" title="Carina&#8211;Sagittarius Arm">Carina&#8211;Sagittarius Arm</a> (the local portions of which are toward the <a href="/wiki/Galactic_Center" title="Galactic Center">Galactic Center</a>) and the <a href="/wiki/Perseus_Arm" title="Perseus Arm">Perseus Arm</a> (the local portion of which is the main outer-most arm and one of two major arms of the galaxy). \n</p><p>Long thought to be a minor structure, namely a "spur" between the two arms mentioned, evidence was presented in mid 2013 that our arm might be a branch of the Perseus Arm, or possibly an independent arm segment.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>\n</p><p>Within the arm, the Solar System is close to its inner rim, in a relative cavity in the arm\'s <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Interstellar_Medium" title="Interstellar Medium">Interstellar Medium</a> known as the <a href="/wiki/Local_Bubble" title="Local Bubble">Local Bubble</a>, about halfway along the arm\'s length, approximately 8,000 parsecs (26,000 light-years) from the Galactic Center.\n</p>\n<div class="toc" id="toc"><input class="toctogglecheckbox" id="toctogglecheckbox" role="button" style="display:none" type="checkbox"/><div
