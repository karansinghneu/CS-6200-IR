to navigation</a>\n<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>\n<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Jorge de Menezes</b> (c. 1498 &#8211; 1537)  was a <a href="/wiki/Portugal" title="Portugal">Portuguese</a> explorer, who in 1526&#8211;27 landed on the islands of <a href="/wiki/Schouten_Islands" title="Schouten Islands">Biak</a> (<a href="/wiki/Cenderawasih_Bay" title="Cenderawasih Bay">Cenderawasih Bay</a>), whilst he awaited the passing of the monsoon season, and on the northern coasts of the <a href="/wiki/Bird%27s_Head_Peninsula" title="Bird\'s Head Peninsula">Bird\'s Head Peninsula</a>, calling the region <i>Ilhas dos Papuas</i>. He is thus credited with the European discovery of <a href="/wiki/New_Guinea" title="New Guinea">New Guinea</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>\n</p><p>Jorge de Menezes was the Portuguese Governor of the <a href="/wiki/Maluku_Islands" title="Maluku Islands">Moluccas</a> from 1527 until 1530, residing on <a href="/wiki/Ternate" title="Ternate">Ternate</a>. While in office, he plundered a Spanish fort on <a href="/wiki/Tidore" title="Tidore">Tidore</a>, poisoned the sultan of Ternate and committed atrocities against the local population. Subsequently he was arrested and sent to <a href="/wiki/Portuguese_India" title="Portuguese India">Portuguese India</a>. After his return to Portugal he was banished to the <a href="/wiki/Colonial_Brazil" title="Colonial Brazil">colony of Brazil</a>, where he died in combat against Indians in 1537.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="References">References</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jorge_de_Menezes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: References">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<div class="reflist" style="list-style-type: decimal;">\n<div class="mw-references-wrap"><ol class="references">\n<li id="cite_note-1"><span class="mw-cite-backlink"><b><a href="#cite_ref-1">^</a></b></span> <span class="reference-text"><cite class="citation book">Kratoska, Paul H.
