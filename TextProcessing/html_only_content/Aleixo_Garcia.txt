known in spanish as alejo garca d 1525 paraguay was a portuguese explorer and conquistador who explored the rio de la plata in service to spain and later paraguay and bolivia.nhe was possibly a member of the failed expedition of juan daz de sols seeking to find a passage from the atlantic to the pacific ocean after reaching the mouths of the uruguay and paran it was apparent that the rio de la plata was not such a strait at this point sols was killed on testimony of his crew by cannibal indians variously identified as the charra or guaran and his lieutenants opted to return to spain.non their return some of their boats were shipwrecked off santa catarina in present-day brazil among the 11 or 18 spanish and portuguese survivors was aleixo garcia a portuguese adventurer who had previously made contact with the guaran with them were also the portuguese sailors henrique montes a veteran of the gonalo coelho and amerigo vespucci expeditions and francisco pacheco and the spanish sailor mechior ramirez alejo de ledesma and francisco chaves were also among them melchior ramires in turn would assist and guide cristvo jacques on his voyage of exploration to ro de la plata and the parana river in 1521 returning again to santa catarina.naleixo garcia traveled inland living among the guarans who knew of the network of trails called peabiru that covered this part of south america while there he heard tales of a white king who lived to the west ruling cities of incomparable riches and splendor.nafter eight years garcia had gathered enough men and supplies to attempt a voyage to the land of the white king marching towards the west using the trail network peabiru his company discovered a great waterfall credit for the discovery of the iguazu falls is usually given to the governor alvar nez cabeza de vaca and historian efram cardozo asserts garcia crossed the paran at the smaller waterfall called saltos del monday.nin 1524 and 1525 the small group four or five men or more than a dozen depending on the sources recruited an army of about 2,000 local guaran soldiers from the vicinity of asuncin as reinforcement to invade the promising new land they then entered the chaco a rough semi-desert region garcia was the first european to cross the chaco and even managed to penetrate the outer defenses of the inca empire on the hills of the andes in present-day bolivia he was the first european to do so accomplishing this eight years before francisco pizarro.ngarcia looted an impressive booty of silver when the army of huayna cpac arrived to challenge him garcia then retreated with the spoils only to be assassinated by his indian allies near san pedro on the paraguay river.nthe indians however spared the life of his son who was the first paraguayan mestizo news of this excursion into incan territory later distracted sebastian cabot from his expedition to the east indies which could have resulted in the second circumnavigation of the globe after ferdinand magellan causing him to imprison or maroon his lieutenants and remain in the rio de la plata region for several years.nnreferenceseditnnnnnnnn ncategories hidden categories n
