(bottom).</div></div></div>\n<p><b>Cosmic background radiation</b> is <a href="/wiki/Electromagnetic_radiation" title="Electromagnetic radiation">electromagnetic radiation</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Big_Bang" title="Big Bang">Big Bang</a>.  The origin of this radiation depends on the region of the <a href="/wiki/Electromagnetic_spectrum" title="Electromagnetic spectrum">spectrum</a> that is observed. One component is the <a href="/wiki/Cosmic_microwave_background" title="Cosmic microwave background">cosmic microwave background</a>. This component is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Redshifted" title="Redshifted">redshifted</a> <a href="/wiki/Photon" title="Photon">photons</a> that have freely streamed from an <a href="/wiki/Recombination_(cosmology)" title="Recombination (cosmology)">epoch when the Universe became transparent</a> for the first time to radiation. Its discovery and detailed observations of its properties are considered one of the major confirmations of the Big Bang. The discovery (by chance in 1965) of the cosmic background radiation suggests that the early universe was dominated by a radiation field, a field of extremely high temperature and pressure.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>\n</p><p>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sunyaev%E2%80%93Zel%27dovich_effect" title="Sunyaev&#8211;Zel\'dovich effect">Sunyaev&#8211;Zel\'dovich effect</a> shows the phenomena of radiant cosmic background radiation interacting with "electron" clouds distorting the spectrum of the radiation.\n</p><p>There is also background radiation in the <a href="/wiki/Infrared" title="Infrared">infrared</a>, <a href="/wiki/X-ray" title="X-ray">x-rays</a>, etc., with different causes, and they can sometimes be resolved into an individual source. See <a href="/wiki/Cosmic_infrared_background" title="Cosmic infrared background">cosmic infrared background</a> and <a href="/wiki/X-ray_background" title="X-ray background">X-ray background</a>. See also <a href="/wiki/Cosmic_neutrino_background" title="Cosmic neutrino background">cosmic neutrino background</a> and <a href="/wiki/Extragalactic_background_light" title="Extragalactic background light">extragalactic background light</a>.\n</p>\n<div class="toc" id="toc"><input class="toctogglecheckbox" id="toctogglecheckbox" role="button" style="display:none" type="checkbox"/><div
