There have been three unsuccessful dedicated missions and many proposals. Because of the proximity of the Mars moons to Mars, any mission to them may also be considered a mission to Mars from some perspectives.\n</p><p>There have been at least three proposals in the United None of these missions were successful: Fobos 1 failed en route to Mars, Fobos 2 failed shortly before landing, and Fobos-Grunt never left <a href="/wiki/Low_Earth_orbit" title="Low Earth orbit">low Earth orbit</a>.\n</p>\n<table class="wikitable sortable" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 0;">\n<tbody><tr>\n<th>Launched mission</th>\n<th>Target</th>\n<th>Reference\n</th></tr>\n<tr>\n<td><a href="/wiki/Phobos_1" title="Phobos 1">Phobos 1</a></td>\n<td>Phobos</td>\n<td>\n</td></tr>\n<tr>\n<td><a href="/wiki/Phobos_2" title="Phobos 2">Phobos 2</a></td>\n<td>Phobos</td>\n<td>\n</td></tr>\n<tr>\n<td><a href="/wiki/Fobos-Grunt" title="Fobos-Grunt">Fobos-Grunt</a></td>\n<td>Phobos</td>\n<td>\n</td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p>Missions sent to the Martian system have returned data on Phobos and Deimos and missions specifically dedicated to the moons are a subset of missions Mars that often include dedicated goals to acquire data about these moons.
